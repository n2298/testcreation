== Description ==

Bux is the best payment gateway for online sellers. Sell products online, generate payment codes via Messenger app, and transfer funds to your bank. 

Bux allows your website to accept payments from Over-the-counter channels (7-Eleven, Cebuana Pawnshop, LBC, Palawan, SM Dept Stores, Bayad Center, etc).
Bux also allows you to accept paymetns from Online Banking channels such as UnionBank, BPI and RCBC.

This Plugin integrates your online store to Bux Payment Platform.

To know more visit Bux's website or Facebook Page:

https://bux.ph/
